What It Is

A showcase with intention, connection, and creative expression.

Temple of Expression is a family-friendly, community-centered performance evening that blends live art with intention, connection, and creative expression.

Performers of all experience levels share short acts such as dance, music, spoken word, theater, and more in a space designed to feel welcoming, uplifting, and fun for both kids and adults alike.

This is not just a traditional talent showcase

We are intending that it will be a place to express, be seen and witnessed within a supportive community that values authenticity, creativity and belonging.

The space is held with care, encouraging:

  • Authentic expression over perfection
  • Support and encouragement over judgment
  • A sense of presence, connection, and community

For Performers

Interested in performing?

Open to first-time and experienced performers.

Whether you have been on stage for years or are just beginning, you are welcome here.

Solo or collaborative acts are welcome, including parent and child performances. All performances must be appropriate for a family-friendly audience and must be 5 minutes in duration or less.

Performance Submissions Questions?

Limited spaces available.

We are looking for (5 min. time slot at most):

  • A dance or movement piece
  • A song or musical performance
  • A poem, story, or spoken word
  • A short theatrical piece
  • Any creative expression that feels authentic to you

About the Host

Chris Young-Ginzburg is an intuitive healer, sound healer, and performer with over 20 years of experience in dance, theater, and creative expression.

She creates spaces that blend performance and healing, inviting people to reconnect with their voice, body, and authentic self.

She is the creator of Sound & Voice Activation, a class designed to help individuals unlock their voice and express themselves more freely.
