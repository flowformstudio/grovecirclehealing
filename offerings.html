Offerings

Every offering is designed to help you return to yourself, at your own pace, in your own way.

01

Sound Healing

Personalized sound experiences that guide the nervous system into deep rest, emotional release, and lasting restoration. Using crystal singing bowls, voice, and intuitive reading, each session is uniquely tailored to your needs.

Sound healing is available as private one-on-one sessions, small group sound baths, and custom immersive experiences for events and retreats.

  • Private 60 or 90-minute sessions
  • Small group sound baths (up to 10)
  • Distance healing sessions available
  • Nervous system regulation & deep rest

02

Sound & Voice
Activation

Reconnect with your voice through breath, movement, toning, and playful expression in a safe, non-judgmental space. This work is not about performance — it's about remembering that your voice is already whole.

Voice activation workshops are offered as group experiences, workshops, and corporate team sessions that open creativity, release tension, and build presence.

  • Group workshops (2–3 hours)
  • Corporate team experiences
  • Breath, toning & embodied expression
  • No musical experience necessary
