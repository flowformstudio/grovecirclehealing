What Is Voice Activation?

Voice Activation is an embodied practice that helps you reconnect with your natural voice — not for performance, but for expression, healing, and self-trust.Rather than focusing on “singing correctly,” this work invites sound to emerge organically through breath, movement, and presence.Since offering sound healing in its many forms since 2009, I’ve discovered something essential: play creates liberation.Drawing from my background in theatre, movement, and clown training, I intentionally hold a space of curiosity, imagination, and playfulness. When the voice is met with lightness instead of pressure, people often experience unexpected breakthroughs — release, confidence, and a sense of freedom that feels both joyful and deeply grounding.In both group classes and one-on-one sessions, I create a safe, non-judgmental container where sound becomes a tool for exploration rather than performance. Through simple vocal play, intuitive sound, guided meditation, and gentle movement, the voice is invited to move freely.Voice Activation is for anyone curious about opening their voice, reclaiming play, and discovering what becomes possible when sound moves without fear.
