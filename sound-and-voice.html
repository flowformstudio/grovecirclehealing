The Practice

What Is Voice Activation?

Voice Activation is an embodied practice that helps you reconnect with your natural voice — not for performance, but for expression, healing, and self-trust. Rather than focusing on 'singing correctly,' this work invites sound to emerge organically through breath, movement, and presence.

Since offering sound healing in its many forms since 2009, I've discovered something essential: play creates liberation. Drawing from my background in theatre, movement, and clown training, I intentionally hold a space of curiosity, imagination, and playfulness.

Voice Activation is for anyone curious about opening their voice, reclaiming play, and discovering what becomes possible when sound moves without fear.
