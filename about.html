My Story

Chris Young-Ginzburg

As an Intuitive Healer, Holistic Coach, and Sound Healer, my journey began in 2003 when I stepped onto the path of clairvoyance, receiving formal training at the Berkeley Psychic Institute in California. I later continued my holistic education at The World School of Massage in San Francisco (2006-2009), where I studied energy work modalities and graduated from their Holistic Health Program as a certified life coach.

From 2008 to 2011, I built a successful healing practice specializing in Vibrational Healing Massage, supporting clients through deep trauma, illness, and emotional challenges while guiding profound personal transformation. My work was later recognized in the book Radical Remission and featured in the accompanying documentary series (Episode 3, Joe's Story) by Hay House Publishing.

Over the years, my work naturally evolved to include sound and voice as powerful tools for healing and self expression. Through sound, movement and embodied awareness, I developed a unique approach that helps people reconnect with their bodies, regulate their nervous systems and access deeper levels of clarity and inner guidance.

Today, I share this work through transformational classes, group experiences and my online Grove Circle Membership, where individuals come together to explore sound healing, voice activation and intuitive development in a supportive, like hearted community.

In 2026, my focus is expanding into larger scale events, retreats and speaking engagements, where I bring these teachings to wider audiences, offering practical and experiential tools for personal transformation through energy awareness, sound healing, vocal expression and embodied presence.
