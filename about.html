My Story

Chris Young-Ginzburg

I'm a sound healer, voice activation guide, and intuitive coach deeply committed to helping you remember what your body already knows. My work lives at the intersection of sound, breath, voice, and the ancient intelligence held within each of us.

I've had the privilege of working with individuals, corporations, and healing communities — from intimate private sessions to large group retreats. Every space I hold is guided by the same intention: to help you feel safe enough to return to yourself.

Whether you're experiencing burnout, creative blocks, grief, or simply a longing for stillness, sound and voice offer a doorway back. My role is simply to hold that doorway open for you.

Approach

The Way I Work

01

You Are the Expert

My sessions are never prescriptive. Your body knows what it needs — I simply create the conditions for that knowing to surface. No two sessions are ever the same.

02

Sound as Medicine

Vibration moves through us at a cellular level. Crystal bowls, voice, breath, and silence all carry frequency that can shift the nervous system into states of deep restoration.

03

Voice as Truth

Your voice is one of the most powerful healing instruments available to you. Reclaiming it — in sound, in expression, in creative play — is an act of profound self-reclamation.
